airbnb k listings (CDMX)

Use this tool to get the price distribution of the k nearest listings from a specific location.

Initial conditions have been set, feel free to indicate the latitude, longitude (or click on any part of the map) and select k neighbors to get the distribution of prices in a certain region. ($ = MX Peso)
